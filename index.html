Join us on SLACK

Anyone at LF is welcome to join us.

We're not just techies talking in binary code about machine learning and robots. We are also designers, creatives, curious minded individuals that want to keep moving forward and constantly question how we can do things differently at LF. As a group we hope to come together and figure out ways to reach new levels of awesome, and perhaps think a little differently to traditional approaches.

we love slack

It's a better way for groups of people to communicate

  • Say goodbye to long email chains
  • It is just like whatsapp/wechat... but even more powerful for work
  • Integrates with Google Drive (docs, spreadsheets, hangouts etc) + Microsoft One Drive
  • Other integrations include Dropbox, Asana, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Trello, Twitter + more
  • Voice and video calls between teams are easy
  • Powerful search function within all chats + even searches within attached documents
  • Use GIFs and emoji's to make team communication fun

Find out how our community is using it to break down silos, create new business opportunities and share information more openly.
